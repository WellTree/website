Welcome to WellTree

WellTree is a personalized health and wellness assistant for those at risk of high blood pressure - hypertension, the leading cause of death in the US. üí™üèΩ

The app pulls data from wearable devices and fitness apps. It makes it easy for the user to understand their risk profile; it tracks insights and provides education and actionable recommendations. In addition, users will have access to health and wellness challenges, and a social layer will provide motivation, encouragement, and community.

WellTree is not intended to replace one's doctor. Still, its goal is to empower them with more insights to engage their healthcare providers proactively. You can think of WellTree as a cross between Apple Health, Strava, and Insight Timer
